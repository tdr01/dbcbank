DCB PHONE BANKING

Frequently asked questions

How can I access DCB Phone Banking when I am overseas or abroad?
You will be able to access this facility from anywhere in the globe and at any time just by dialling the number
(0091) (22) 61271000.
Are there any charges for the Phone Banking facility?
This facility is absolutely free for all DCB Bank Account holders
Can I use my ATM Pin instead of verbal authentication at Phone Banking to authenticate my identity.?
Yes, at DCB Bank we are dedicated to maintain the privacy of your account information and ensure that your account details are not shared with any unauthorized person. You can validate your identity by entering your ATM-Pin in conference with our Customer Care Associate or answer few simple verification questions to enable us access your account. At DCB Bank, authentication is further simplified if you call from your registered mobile number.
Can I generate my ATM pin over the phone?
Yes, you need to call our customer care number from your registered mobile number. our customer care associate will assist you with the process.
Can the debit card be reissued to me on Phone Banking?
Yes, the card will be reissued at an additional charge. The card will be sent to your communication address and will reach you within 10 working days.
Can I request a stop payment of cheque over the phone?
Yes, you will be able to stop the payment of either single or multiple cheques over the phone. However if the cheque has already been presented for clearing, we will not be able to accept “stop payment” of the cheque.  We will also not be able to process request for jointly operated accounts
If I have forgotten my Internet Banking Password or my Password is disabled, can you reissue a new password over the phone?
Our customer care associate can reset the password immediately or take your request for new password and you will be sent a new Internet password by post. Alternatively you may also generate the password online by using the Forgot Password option.
Can I know the details of the term deposits that I have with DCB Bank?
Yes, you will be able to get the details of your Term Deposits as and when you require, as well as information on the latest offerings by DCB Bank.
Can I apply for a new product via DCB Phone Banking?
Yes, Phone Banking team will be more than happy to take your details and shall ensure your any product requirement is met
Can I lodge a complaint with Phone Banking?
Yes, Phone banking team will ensure best possible and quick resolution for your complaint. For every complaint your will also be provided with a reference number for future tracking.
Is the statement by fax feature available?
We currently do not provide this feature through Phone Banking
Do I have an option to write to you?
Yes, you may email to us on below email ids.
For domestic / resident customers: customercare@dcbbank.com
For NRI customers:nri@dcbbank.com.
For loan customers:loans@dcbbank.com.

You may also write to us at : "DCB Bank Ltd., P.O.Box No.7643, Malad (West), Mumbai 400064"

At DCB Bank your account security is our top most priority and hence we request you to e-mail us using your registered e-mail ID for account related information.
