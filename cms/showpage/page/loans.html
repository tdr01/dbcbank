LOANS
DCB Home / Micro Home Loans
Schedule of Service and fees for DCB Home Loans and DCB Micro Home Loans
Fee
Loan Processing Fee/Charges (Non-refundable) Upto 2% of the loan amount, subject to a minimum of Rs.5,000/-, which would be payable at the time of loan application (by way of a cheque / PO / DD), balance would be payable prior to disbursement.
Annual Maintenance Charges (PayLess Home Operative Account) Where average utilization of the outstanding loan amount is <70% during the year from the date of full disbursement of the Loan an annual maintenance charge of upto 1% will be charged on principal outstanding amount at the end of year. This will be an annual charge which will be debited from your PayLess Home Operative Account during the following quarter.
Full Prepayment (Foreclosure) Charges
  1. Full Prepayment (Foreclosure) of the loan is not permitted in the first 12 (twelve) months from the date of full disbursement.
  2. No prepayment / foreclosure charges would be levied where the loan is availed on fully variable / floating rate of interest and has completed 12 (twelve) months from the date of full disbursement.
  3. For loans availed under part fixed + part variable rate of interest, on completion of 12 (twelve) months from the date of full disbursement charges plus applicable service tax would be levied to the Borrower on outstanding loan amount including any part prepayment made during the financial year as per gird prescribed below.
Part Prepayment Charges
  1. No part prepayment allowed in the first 6 (six) months from the date of full disbursement.
  2. No part prepayment charges would be levied where the loan is availed on variable / floating rate of interest and has completed 6 (six) month from the date of full disbursement.
  3. For loans availed with part fixed + part variable rate of interest, on completion of 6 (six) months from the date of full disbursement no charges will be levied for part prepayment upto a maximum of 25% of the outstanding loan amount at any time in a financial year. For payment exceeding 25% of the outstanding loan amount, charges as per below grid plus applicable service tax will be levied on the entire amount part prepaid in the given financial year
  4. Part prepayments made during the month, if any will be adjusted towards principal only on the next cycle due date of EMI.
  5. No part prepayment and reduction of EMI is permitted in PayLess Home Loan facility.
